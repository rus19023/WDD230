Site Name: Edge of the Cloud

Domain name
Domain name EdgeOfTheCloud.com chosen from domains.google.com, available as of 1/28/2020 for $12/year.

This page describes the future website at edgeofthecloud.info, which will be the go-to site for weather information for the local areas of Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven and the Storm Center.

It will provide weather and climate data, forecasts and warnings to aid the local communities and people in them to prepare for emergencies, short or long term visits and planning vacations and daily tasks.
Logo and Icons
I just happened to have a project due the same week in another class, Visual Media (COMM 130), that required the design and creation of a set of icons. With a bit of early morning, just woke up inspiration, I decided to create them to use in this course's main project: the weather website. This site-wide header logo is an additional design made from parts of the weather icons, which I will be using for the various weather scenarios in future parts of the site.
