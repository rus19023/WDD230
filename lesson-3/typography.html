Primary Fonts

The primary text fonts are Poppins, Comfortaa, Questrial and sans-serif, stacked in that order. Heading fonts are Comfortaa for its more modern curvy and contrasting look compared to Poppins, to draw attention to the headings and any other emphasized text.
