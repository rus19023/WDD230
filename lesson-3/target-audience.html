Edge of the Cloud

Target Audience

The target audience for this website is any human of age from 5 to 105 who needs to know current and/or future weather conditions to help plan their school, work or play day, or their visit or vacation, or just driving conditions if they are passing through.

It will also be very useful for local businesses who sell any products related to the weather, for example, if they sell umbrellas and it will rain in the next week or so, they may want to do a promotion on their umbrellas. Other non-local companies may want to do targeted marketing to certain local areas that will be having severe weather conditions also.

Users will probably be checking their phones for the weather reports, so it will be important to have a responsive website. The information will need to be available 24 hours a day and updated regularly for those who will be checking often.

Personas

Persona 1

Mary Woodman


Age 7 female who lives in Preston with her mom and 2 younger siblings.

Needs to know the weather forecast for the weekend but Mom doesn't allow her to watch tv on school days.

She needs a way to get the weather prediction so she knows if she can ride her bike to the library on Saturday with her friends. Mom does allow her to use her cell phone to look things up quickly.

Kindergarten

Scenarios

Possible scenarios for use of this site are:

  • General store in Soda Falls is promoting snowshoes because they know the forecast is for 12 inches of snow next week.
  • Computer salesman is driving through Soda Springs on his way to a convention nearby. Should he take another route because there will be heavy rains and flooding?
  • Grandma Reed will be flying to her daughter's home near Preston for the holidays. What clothing should she bring?
  • There is an ice storm coming to the area. What can the Storm Center tell us to help us prepare?
  • Will I be able to go hunting in Preston's woods next weekend?
