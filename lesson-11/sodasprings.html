Soda Springs Geyser

March 16, 2020


Did you know there is only 1 man-made "captive" geyser in the whole world? It's located right here in Soda Springs! Back in 1937, someone wanted a natural source of hot water for their swimming pool, so they started drilling. Just over 300 feet deep, they hit the vein of naturally carbonated (soda) water. This naturally bubbling fountain was discovered in the 1800's when the Oregon Trail became well used.

The geyser has been capped and is now activated by a timer, which allows it to burst upwards every hour on the hour. We have a nice park here now for visitors, too. Nearby is also an active lava flow left over from Monsanto's phosphate mining.
