It's Ice Fishing Weather!

February 4, 2020


There are various lakes and reservoirs where fisherwomen and fishermen like to hang out near Preston, Idaho. Some offer camping too!

Groups that fish in the ice and snow frequent them this time of year. One popular group is Ice Fishing Fools of Idaho

Here are a few of the most popular fishin' holes:

  • Foster Reservoir
  • Twin Lakes Reservoir
  • Lamont Reservoir
  • Treasureton Reservoir
  • Condie Reservoir
  • Bear Lake
